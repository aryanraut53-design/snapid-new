Frequently Asked Questions

Everything users usually ask before printing passport photos.

Is my output print-ready?

Yes. PDFs are generated on A4 at high resolution with configurable spacing and border for common print shops.

What if background removal fails?

The app now fails softly and continues with the original image, so you still get a PDF instead of a hard error.

How many files can I store in history?

There is no app-imposed limit. History is stored in IndexedDB and grows until your browser storage quota is reached.

Can I download old PDFs later?

Yes. Go to the History page to preview, download, or remove any previously generated file.
