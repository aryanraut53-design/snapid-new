About SnapID.Pro

Built to make passport photos simple, trustworthy, and print-ready.

SnapID.Pro helps students, families, and professionals generate clean passport photo sheets in minutes. Our workflow combines guided cropping, AI-assisted background cleanup, smart enhancement, and A4 PDF output tuned for real-world print shops.

Reliability First

The app is engineered with graceful fallbacks so temporary AI-service issues do not block final PDF generation.

Privacy Aware

Security headers, token-gated access, CSRF protection, and session checks are built in to reduce misuse risks.

Print Accuracy

Output is laid out on A4 at 300 DPI with configurable dimensions, spacing, and border so final sheets are press-ready.

How SnapID.Pro Works

1. Upload & Crop

Upload one or many photos and crop with passport ratio guidance.

2. AI Refinement

Background removal and enhancement are applied with automatic mode selection.

3. Quality Checks

Validation and safe defaults keep dimensions, spacing, and copies within sensible limits.

4. Export PDF

Get a downloadable multi-page PDF tailored for straightforward photo printing.

Trust & Transparency

  • Clear status feedback for success, partial AI processing, and recoverable failures.
  • Rate limiting to protect availability and prevent abuse.
  • Defensive backend error handling to avoid vague, silent failures.
