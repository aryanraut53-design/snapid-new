How to Use SnapID.Pro

A practical guide to get a clean result on the first try.

Step 1

Upload a clear photo

Use a front-facing image with proper lighting and simple background for best AI cleanup.

Step 2

Crop precisely

Use the crop tool to center the face and keep chin-to-head ratio passport-friendly.

Step 3

Pick enhancement mode

Auto Detect works for most users. Use Brighten for dark photos and Fix Color for warm/yellow casts.

Step 4

Generate and preview PDF

Click Generate Print Sheet. Review the preview and then download.

Step 5

Use History for later

Every generated PDF is auto-saved in your browser history (IndexedDB), with download/view/delete support.
